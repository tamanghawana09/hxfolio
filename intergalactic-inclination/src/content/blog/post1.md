# Hello World

Welcome to my corner of the internet. This is where I'll share thoughts on design, development, and everything in between — unfiltered, and in my own words.

## Why I started this

Writing is thinking. I've found that putting ideas into words forces clarity in a way that nothing else does. This blog is that exercise, made public.

> The best time to start was yesterday. The second best time is now.

## What to expect

Posts about **Astro**, design systems, and the occasional deep dive into something I've been obsessing over. Sometimes *short notes*, sometimes long essays.

Here's what I'll be writing about:

- Frontend development & Astro
- Design systems and typography  
- Personal projects and experiments
- Things I'm learning in public

## A note on consistency

I'm not promising a schedule. I'm promising honesty — when something is worth writing about, I'll write about it.
